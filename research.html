Mastery of Learning Assessments in early programming courses

In this project, we are investigating a new pedagogical approach in which large, high-stakes exams are substituted with smaller, low stakes, repeatable tests that students can take to demonstrate mastery of a particular programming concept. Our hypothesis is that the new format is more engaging and produces more lasting results in students in early programming classes.

Course Gamification

We are investigating the most effective ways to incorporate games or game concepts in Computer Science courses to see if and how much they help students mastering and retaining the material. At the moment, we are looking at using badges to reward students who have shown mastery of a particular topic (in close connection with our Mastery of Learning study). We believe that the natural human disposition to “collect” will prove to be a powerful tool to increase students’ engagement.
